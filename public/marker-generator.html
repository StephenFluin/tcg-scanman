ArUco Marker Generator for TCG Scanner

Instructions:

  1. Click the "Print Markers" button below
  2. Print the page on white paper (A4 or Letter size)
  3. Place your Pokemon card in the center dashed rectangle
  4. Position the printed page so all 4 markers are visible to your camera
  5. The scanner will automatically detect the card using these markers

Note: These markers use the ARUCO dictionary which is compatible with js-aruco2.
